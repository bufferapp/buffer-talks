# What Is Atomic Design?

**The Five Levels**

- Atoms
- Molecules
- Organisms
- Templates
- Pages

---

# Atoms

- Building block of an application
- Work in a number of contexts
- Often not useful on their own

- Groups of atoms bonded together

- Built for re-use
